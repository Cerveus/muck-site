Welcome to Muck — Ottawa's restaurant deals app.

This is an early version. Use it the way you'd use any app to find something to eat tonight. Browse around, see what catches your eye, save things you like.

A few things to know:
- Only Ottawa restaurants right now
- Sign in with Apple to save deals
- No dark mode yet
- If a deal looks wrong (bad price, closed restaurant, wrong hours), tap the flag icon on the deal — that helps us a lot

If something feels off, broken, or annoying, hit the Feedback tab and tell us. Screenshots welcome.
